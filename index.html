Background

Twenty years making things land.

I started in sports engineering at Loughborough and have spent two decades developing consumer products across fitness, wellness, apparel and infant care. I am a named inventor on patents at every employer. None of those are mine. KettleCross is.

I build a lot because I built myself a persistent-memory system first. It runs about 500KB of structured markdown on OneDrive, routed through Filesystem MCP. It is why this portfolio moves at this pace with one person at the wheel.

AI does the exploration. Professionals validate and execute when decisions get expensive. That rule sits behind every venture here.

Portfolio is the risk strategy. Ownership is a mechanism for agency, not an end in itself.
